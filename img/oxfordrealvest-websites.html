About Oxford DredgeVest

Pioneering investment in dredging activities in Nigeria

Oxford DredgeVest is an elaborate investment Application Technology created to bring about an easy, robust and secure channel to profit from the Dredging Industry in Nigeria.

Dredging is a routine necessity in waterways around the world because sedimentation, the natural process of sand and silt washing downstream gradually fills channels and harbors. The depth of navigation channels, anchorages, or berthing areas needs to be increased to ensure the safe passage of boats and ships. Vessels require a certain amount of water in order to float and not touch bottom. Since massive ships carry the bulk of the goods imported into Nigeria, dredging plays a vital role in the nation’s economy.

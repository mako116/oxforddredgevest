Terminating Investment

It is ideal customers are aware on the procedure for termination of investments on Oxford DredgeVest. Below are the criteria for termination of investments.

For personal or business reasons, either of the parties is not allowed to seek for termination of the agricultural business scheme herein BEFORE OR ON the period of 90 (Ninety) days, such intention shall be communicated to the other party in writing at least 3 (THREE) MONTHS before the date of Such termination.
In respect of notice of termination of Agricultural Business Scheme by the Investor before the due date, the Investor shall agree to waive the total interest accrued on the Investment amount being the penalty fee.
In the event the investor gives a notice of termination of the agricultural business scheme herein within 6 months of the commencement date of the agreement, the said investor shall lose all interest accrued and 10% (Ten) of the deposited investment capital as well. The refund sum less 10% (Ten) of the investment capital shall be paid by the Company in one installment at least 14 (Fourteen) working days upon receipt of request for termination. After 6-9 months of the commencement date, 5% of the investment capital will be deducted and the return on investment forfeited, but if later than 9(Nine) months after the commencement only the total interest will be forfeited.
The company shall pay all accrued capital and interest of the investors deposit only on the due date. At the event the due date falls within weekend or public holiday, the investor shall give 5(Five) additional working days for the company to make payment.
The investor shall have the right to call and check on the update of the Agricultural business scheme from time to time.
